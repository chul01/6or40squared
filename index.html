Work

The important thing of test prep is learning. You must find ask yourself why you answered what and find out why you made every mistake you've made that you care about. The SAT and the ACT are about how smart you are, but they are more about how well you can take a test, and most of all, they are testing how much you can improve yourself. This is the art of test-taking.
